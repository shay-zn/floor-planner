How to Use Floor Planner

  1. Upload a floor plan image or PDF
  2. Add people using "Add Person" or "Bulk Add" buttons
  3. Drag people from the list to place them on the floor plan
  4. Drag placed people around to rearrange seating
  5. Click the colored circle or paint brush icon to change colors
  6. Export the final arrangement as an image

Tips:

  • Double-click a placed person to remove them from the floor plan
  • Use clear, readable floor plan images for best results
  • Save your work with "Save State" and load it later with "Load State"
  • Use bulk add to quickly add multiple people with the same color
